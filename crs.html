Power E Sharing Dreams and Kindness

Power E Modular Co., Ltd., together with their sponsor Idea Four Co., Ltd., organized the 8th annual 'Sharing Dreams and Kindness' event at Ban Thong Chai Lang School in Tak Province. The event provided essential school supplies and learning materials to support education for children, promoting educational opportunities and creating a better future for youth.
